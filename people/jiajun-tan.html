Jiajun Tan, PhD

Postdoctoral Researcher · Proteasome Biology and Targeted Protein Degradation
Jiajun.Tan@PennMedicine.upenn.edu
LinkedIn

Jiajun Tan earned his PhD in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences and joined the Busino Lab in 2025. His research focuses on proteasome-dependent protein degradation, including both ubiquitin-dependent and ubiquitin-independent pathways, in the context of hematologic cancers.

In the Busino Lab, Jiajun combines biochemical assays, flow cytometry, CRISPR-Cas9 screens, and small-molecule screens to define mechanisms of targeted protein degradation. His work aims to identify druggable nodes within these pathways and determine how small molecule-induced degradation can disrupt oncogenic networks, creating new therapeutic opportunities for blood cancers.

Fun fact: When he is not in the lab, Jiajun enjoys hiking or exploring the city on foot.
